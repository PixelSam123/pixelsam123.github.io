New Website Released!

08 July 2025

See what's new and what's moved in my 2025 redesign of my personal webpage!

What’s the motivation?

It’s been nearly two years since my last personal website design, and a lot has changed with me, my needs, and my endeavours since then. My previous design had a playful design which reflected my playful early university days and was aimed at showcasing personal projects. Since then, I’ve taken on multiple jobs and volunteering opportunities, giving me plenty of experience and a chance to learn important lessons about myself and other people. I am now in a position where my aim is to provide a good range of services and give impact through my works.

You’ll notice that this site has moved away from the purely playful design into a more professional look. The heading font has also changed. I’ve tried to preserve some playfulness where I can, by adding in splashes of colors here and there. I’ve also tried to emphasize images and content whenever I can, letting control components get out of the way. The navbar has been moved down and no longer stretches across the length of the page, for example. I hope this results in a tidier design that aligns more with sensible UI design.

If you want to check my old website, I’ve fired up a Vercel site just for that. Visit this link so that you can make the comparison for yourself!

What’s new?

New Hero header in the homepage

This is a big emphasis of this redesign. This is a free place for me to put really nice pictures and inform you all about important stuff I’m involved with (and maybe even opportunities for you in the future).

New Services section

This is the section where I offer my available services and give contacts for where you can reach out to me about them. A preview is available through a carousel of cards in the homepage, which can redirect you to the main pages showing you my portfolio for each category of service I provide - Professional, Semi-Professional and Hobby services.

What’s moved?

Most content that was previously here, is actually still here. My social media links are still on the homepage, just moved up and redesigned. The Markdown repository previews have been moved into the Professional Services section of the site. Same goes with my GitHub markdown preview, as my GitHub markdown only needs to represent the programming part of my services.

What’s gone?

The large “featured repository” banner has been removed, making way for a future redesign where I feature my newest works.
